Property & Registry

For a standard property sale deed registration, you will need the original title deed of the seller, an Encumbrance Certificate (EC) from the sub-registrar's office, identity proof (Aadhaar/PAN) for both parties, a passport-size photograph, property tax receipts, and the no-objection certificate if applicable. We provide a complete personalised checklist after your initial consultation.

Once all documents are in order, registration at the Kolkata sub-registrar's office typically takes 1–3 working days. Delays usually occur due to incomplete paperwork — which we help you avoid entirely.

Stamp duty in West Bengal is generally 6% of the property value (5% stamp duty + 1% additional), plus 1% registration fee. Rates may vary based on property type and buyer category (women buyers receive a concession). We calculate the exact amount for your specific case.

Affidavits & POA

A Power of Attorney (POA) is a legal document that authorises someone to act on your behalf in legal, financial, or property matters. It is commonly required for NRIs managing property in India, elderly individuals who cannot be present in person, and businesses appointing authorised representatives.

Yes. An NRI can execute a POA at the Indian Embassy or Consulate in their country of residence, which is then sent to India for use. We assist NRI clients through the complete process remotely.
